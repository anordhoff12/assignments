January 10, 2017 - Salt Lake City

Programmers A Bit Out Of Sorts After Losing Ending Bracket In March Madness

SALT LAKE CITY - Local programmers are a bit out of sorts after getting wrangled into "playing a brackets game" that had nothing to do with the "usual types of arrays," one programmer stated.

In their first ever attempt to participate in March Madness, after being lured in by the potential use of brackets, programmers quickly realized it wasn't what they were hoping for.

Local computer clubs have posted warning on their group Facebook page, hoping to save fellow non-athletic coding friends from a similar, embarrassing fate. Read more of this story on page 10.
